**Sengoku Chronicles: The Legend of Yukimura**

> *「真田は死なぬ。竹のように撓む」*
> *"A Sanada never dies. We bend like bamboo."*
> — Sanada Masayuki, as imagined in this game

## About

You **are** Sanada Nobushige — the man history would call Yukimura.

A narrative RPG tracing his life from boyhood in Shinano Province (1575) to his last impossible charge at the Battle of Tennōji (1615). Every major battle is real. Every political turn is real. The inner voice, the moments of doubt — those are fiction filling the space history left blank.

The player does not choose Yukimura's fate. History already did.
The player chooses **how he faces it**.

## How to Play

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. No installation. No server. No account.
